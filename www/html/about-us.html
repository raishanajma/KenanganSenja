About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Kenangansenja is a retail coffee startup that wants to serve high quality coffee to its customers. The name Kenangansenja is taken from the word Memories which is reminiscing about the good old days, relaxing the mind, and keeping away the burden of thoughts, while the word twilight is the evening. We want our existence to create a new standard for quality coffee for consumers.

Leveraging our network and experience, we use the latest technology and blend the best coffee. Coffee from selected farmers, high quality coffee beans are processed and roasted to perfection and forwarded by reliable baristas. Who is always passionate about preparing a special glass of happiness for you.
